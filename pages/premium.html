Frequently Asked Questions

How does the premium membership work?

Premium membership gives you exclusive benefits including extra discounts, free delivery, priority access to books, and no advance payment requirements. Choose from monthly, quarterly, or annual plans based on your needs.

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your premium membership anytime from your account settings. Your benefits will continue until the end of your current billing period.

What happens to my free rentals if I don't use them?

Free rentals are valid for your subscription period. Quarterly plan offers 2 free rentals valid for 3 months, and annual plan offers 5 free rentals valid for 12 months. Unused rentals don't carry forward.

Is the discount applicable on all books?

Yes! Premium discounts are applicable on all books available on VidyaShare, including new arrivals and exclusive collections.
