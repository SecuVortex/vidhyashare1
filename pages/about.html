About VidyaShare

Revolutionizing education through affordable book sharing

Our Mission

At VidyaShare, we believe that knowledge should be accessible to all. We're on a mission to make education more affordable by creating India's largest community of students who share, rent, and sell textbooks.

Founded by students, for students, we understand the financial burden of expensive textbooks. That's why we've created a platform where you can rent books at just 15% of their MRP per month, helping you save up to 85% on your educational expenses.
